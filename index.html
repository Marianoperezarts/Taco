Simple Taco Recipe

A Delicious & Easy Meal, Perfect For Any Occasion!

Preparation times

  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked protein (beef, chicken, pork, or beans)
  • ½ cup shredded cheese (cheddar, cotija, or queso fresco)
  • ½ cup chopped vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, onions, or peppers)
  • ¼ cup salsa or hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil (for heating tortillas)
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, guacamole, cilantro, lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients: Chop vegetables, shred cheese, and warm up the protein.
  2. Heat tortillas: In a pan over medium heat, warm tortillas with a little oil until soft.
  3. Assemble tacos: Place your protein on each tortilla, followed by cheese, vegetables, and toppings.
  4. Serve and enjoy: Garnish with lime and cilantro, and enjoy hot!
